Hi all,

The [<b>x-kom</b>](https://www.x-kom.pl/) is a company which everyone know.  
Currently, they are one of the biggest retailers of electronic articles in Poland.  

A few weeks ago I looked closer at the mobile application for Android and iOS operating systems.  

I found 3 bugs in both applications.  
- In the first issue, I can not add the article to the cart when I am not sign in.  
- In the second, the price from which is discount is equaled 0.00.  
- In the third, not all articles have information about sold amounts.

I sent the request with the screenshots to [<b>x-kom</b>](https://www.x-kom.pl/) via e-mail.  

I received only information about the received request,  
and no message about fixed function.  

The life went forward and sometime after sent request, the mobile applications for both operating systems received updates.  

After these updates, I got something from [<b>x-kom</b>](https://www.x-kom.pl/) - they changed the minimal version of iOS from 12.0 to 13.0.  
So, now I can't get access via an application from my iPhone 5S - but Apple still publish security updates, 
so I can think this model is still supported.

Also, I do not understand one thing:  
if they do not allow me to have access to the store - why they still sent a push notification to me about new promotions?  

It is everything that I have for today.  
Everyone is a blacksmith of own fate.  
Mateusz.
